## Structural finding (applies to every module)
The *Mathematics for Physics* chapters teach the math **as math** and do not mention QM. So in every module the **QM-bridge** (section B "where the series uses it" and section D "QM-specific pitfalls") had to be authored fresh. That bridge IS the companion's value — but it means drafting is real writing, not transcription.

## Math the source books do NOT cover (must be authored for the companion)
- **Tensor products / Schmidt decomposition** (M-16, M-07) — no source chapter; M-16 authored from Nielsen & Chuang / Preskill / Peres.
- **Complex matrices, Hermitian conjugate, Pauli matrices, trace, Kronecker product** (M-12) — MfP1 Ch 10 covers only real matrices (~30% of what M-12 needs).
- **Continuous spectra / generalized eigenfunctions** and **functions of operators** (e^{−iHt/ℏ} via spectral decomposition) (M-08, M-09) — absent from the finite-dimensional sources; needed for time evolution.
- **Superoperators / Lindblad** for IV·6 (M-09) — not in any source.
- **Complex Fourier series** (M-05) and the **Gaussian integral derivation** (M-02) — used constantly in QM, not derived in source.
- **Spherical Laplacian** (give without derivation, M-10); **associated Laguerre / spherical Bessel** detail (M-11) — only sketched in source.
