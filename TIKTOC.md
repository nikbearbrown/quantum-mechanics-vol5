## Concept
Never adopted alone. A just-in-time math refresher indexed to Volumes 1–4: for any QM chapter, the one or two tools it leans on, each a compressed, physics-motivated refresher condensed from *Mathematics for Physics* Vols 1–2.

## Design rules
1. Every module is keyed to a source chapter in the two *Mathematics for Physics* books — it re-derives, it does not invent. 2. Every module opens with why *this* quantum chapter needs *this* math. 3. Modules are short by contract. 4. The deepest cases point on to Shankar or Cohen-Tannoudji.
