# Quantum Mechanics Vol. 5 — Math for Quantum Physics

**Author:** Nik Bear Brown

---

## One-Sentence Pitch
The optional companion to the *Quantum Mechanics* series: when a student hits a chapter whose math they are shaky on, they read one short module — not a full math book — derive just the tool that chapter needs, and return to the physics.

## The Argument
Most QM books either assume the math or front-load it in one dense chapter the student reads once and forgets. Both fail the student who is fine until Chapter 9 and then meets Laguerre polynomials cold. This companion is the third way: a math layer **indexed to the quantum chapters**, so the refresher arrives exactly when the chapter needs it and is exactly as long as that need. Each module condenses a chapter of *Mathematics for Physics* (Vols 1–2) and points there for depth.

## The Reader
A student in any of Volumes 1–4 who wants a quick, physics-motivated reminder of a specific tool without a full-book detour.

## How to use it
Open the Mirror Index in the series TIKTOC at the QM chapter you are on; it names the one or two modules to skim.
